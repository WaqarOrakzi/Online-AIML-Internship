## Key Findings

### 1. Reading had highest average score
- Reading: 69.17
- Writing: 68.05
- Math: 66.09

Math was the most difficult subject.

---

### 2. Test preparation improved scores
Students who completed test prep scored higher in all subjects.

- Math improvement: 8.77%
- Reading improvement: 11.06%
- Writing improvement: 15.37%

---

### 3. Gender-based trends
Male students performed better in math.

Female students performed better in:
- Reading
- Writing
